About Bags By Eve

Crafting elegance for the modern woman

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Bags By Eve emerged from a passion for combining timeless elegance with modern functionality. Our founder, Evelyn, noticed a gap in the market for high-quality, stylish bags that could seamlessly transition from day to night, from the boardroom to a dinner date.

Each bag in our collection is thoughtfully designed with the modern woman in mind. We understand that your bag is more than just an accessory - it's a companion that carries your essentials, your dreams, and your style.

Our Craftsmanship

We partner with skilled artisans who share our commitment to quality and attention to detail. From the selection of premium materials to the final stitch, every bag undergoes rigorous quality checks to ensure it meets our high standards.

Sustainability

We're committed to responsible manufacturing practices. Our vegan leather collection uses innovative, eco-friendly materials, and we're continuously working to reduce our environmental footprint throughout our supply chain.
