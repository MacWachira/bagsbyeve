About Bags By Eve

Our story and commitment to quality

Made for the Modern Woman

Bags By Love was born from a passion for creating beautiful, functional bags that empower women in their daily lives. Each design is carefully crafted with attention to detail, quality materials, and the modern woman's needs in mind.

Founded in 2018 by fashion enthusiast Sarah Wanjiku, our brand has grown from a small boutique in Nairobi to a beloved name across Kenya. We believe that every woman deserves to carry a bag that not only complements her style but also supports her busy lifestyle with practical features and durable construction.

Our Journey

What started as a passion project in a small Nairobi studio has blossomed into a thriving business that employs over 20 local artisans. We take pride in our Kenyan roots while drawing inspiration from global fashion trends.

Every Bags By Love creation tells a story of craftsmanship, empowerment, and style. We work with skilled artisans who bring generations of expertise to each stitch, ensuring that every bag is not just an accessory, but a piece of art that stands the test of time.

Our Passion

We're passionate about creating bags that combine style, functionality, and durability. Our design philosophy centers around creating pieces that transition seamlessly from day to night, work to weekend.

Our Community

We value our customers and strive to build a community of confident, stylish women. Through our social media platforms and events, we connect with our customers to understand their needs and preferences.

Our Impact

We're committed to ethical manufacturing and sustainable practices. We use eco-friendly materials where possible and ensure fair wages and safe working conditions for all our team members.
